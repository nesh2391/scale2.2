Backlog Refinement Refine and Create Classes

Product Backlog Refinement is the act of adding detail, estimates, and order to items in the Product Backlog. Refinement usually consumes no more than 10% of the capacity of the Development Team.

Release Management Manage Releases

Releases help you decide what to deliver (release) to customers/users. Releases help you build a continuous release stream (release train) that helps you set expectations and plan ahaead.

Sprint Management The core of your adgile team's ability to drive value

Scrum Sprint is a repeatable fixed time-box during which a "Done" product of the highest possible value is created. Sprint lies at the core of the Sprint agile methodology and can be thought of as an event which wraps all other Scrum events like Daily Scrums, Scrum Review and Sprint Retrospective.
